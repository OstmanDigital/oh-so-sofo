2. What personal data we process

We aim to minimise the processing of personal data on our website. The site does not actively collect personal data and has no forms, accounts or newsletters.

The following processing may occur:

  • Google Maps (optional): If you choose to display the map on our contact page, Google Maps is loaded. This sends your IP address and certain browser information to Google Ireland Limited. The map is not loaded until you actively click "Show map".
  • Email contact: If you email us at info@ohsosofo.se, we process the information you provide (e.g. name and email address) in order to respond to your enquiry. Data is not retained longer than necessary.

3. Purposes and legal basis

Processing Purpose Legal basis
Google Maps (after consent) Display an interactive map so visitors can easily find the shop Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR)
Email communication Respond to questions and enquiries from visitors Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR)

4. Recipients of personal data

We do not share your personal data with third parties beyond the following:

  • Google Ireland Limited (Google Maps) — if you choose to activate the map. Google may process data outside the EU/EEA. Google is certified under EU Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs). See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

5. Retention periods

Email correspondence is kept for as long as the matter is active and thereafter for up to 12 months, unless there is a reason to retain it longer (e.g. for accounting purposes).

6. Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification — request that inaccurate data is corrected.
  • Erasure — request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten"), where no legal grounds require its retention.
  • Restriction — request that processing is restricted in certain circumstances.
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interest.
  • Data portability — request your data in a machine-readable format.

To exercise your rights, contact us at info@ohsosofo.se. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Right to lodge a complaint

If you believe we are processing your personal data unlawfully, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Swedish supervisory authority, Integritetsskyddsmyndigheten (IMY).

8. Cookies

This website does not set any tracking cookies of its own. If you choose to display Google Maps, Google may set cookies in accordance with its cookie policy. We store your choice to show the map in the browser's localStorage solely to avoid showing the consent prompt on future visits.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The date of the most recent update is shown at the top of this page. We recommend checking back periodically to stay informed.
