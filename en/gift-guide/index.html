Gift Guide

Recommended selections

We are happy to help you find the right gift.

Solstickan Design

Oh So SOFO is honoured to offer a broad range of Solstickan Design products. With a purchase of any item with the official Solstickan emblem, you help to support the Solstickan Foundation, which, since its founding in 1936, has provided funds toward aiding efforts to improve the lives and conditions toward children and the elderly. The instantly recognisable Solstickan boy is one of the most widely known emblems in Sweden, and has been a trusted mark for high quality products in support of the Foundation, from its beginnings with the humble safety match, and now includes designer products for the kitchen, home decor, outdoor grilling, and more.

Gourmet Foods

Here you will find Swedish made gourmet delights with the true taste of Sweden — honey sourced from the rooftops of the greater Stockholm area supporting biodiversity, jams and jellies made from fruits and berries picked from Swedish farms and forests, chocolates produced in the far north of Sweden which are free from lactose, gluten, dairy, nuts, or soy, an ever changing selection that will delight your tastebuds. We also will find BIO and organic offerings that we consider to be truly outstanding sourced from Europe, but chosen specifically to complement and enhance traditional Swedish cuisine and traditions.

Artisanal Crafts

Swedish artisanal craftsmanship at its best is showcased in our offering of leather goods, accessories, gift items and more. Handmade items with the greatest attention to detail, made to be long lasting and timeless.

Clothing & Fabric Items

We carry a wide assortment of Swedish designed clothing and fabric items, ranging from Solstickan design kitchen and home furnishings, to T-shirts designed by small family businesses in Stockholm and other parts of Sweden, and other clothing items made and designed with the Swedish customer in mind.

Face & Body Care

Refreshing and calming care for the face and body, with a varying selection of fine offerings. We endeavour to offer high quality selections sourced from Sweden and other parts of Scandinavia that will address any skincare regime.
