About us

A shop with warmth and personality

Oh So Sofo is a gift and specialties boutique with a focus on products designed or produced in and around Stockholm. The shop has the broadest available range of Solstickan Design items, along with handcrafted items made locally, specialty organic food and skincare products, along with accessories for the home and to wear.

  • 🎁
    Curated assortment Every product is chosen with thought and taste.
  • 🤝
    Personal service We help you find the right gift for the right person.
  • 🏳️‍🌈
    Inclusive environment Everyone is welcome — whoever you are and whoever you love.
  • 📍
    Heart of SoFo Beautifully located in one of Stockholm's cosiest neighbourhoods.
